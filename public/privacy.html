Trust & safeguards

Privacy policy

Your travel preferences are yours. Sarathi is designed with data minimization and transparency first.

What we collect: Only the trip inputs you provide (destination, dates, interests, constraints) and minimal session data to keep your experience consistent. We do not sell or share this data with advertisers.

How it is used: Inputs are processed solely to generate itineraries and suggestions. Aggregated, anonymized insights may be used to improve product quality, never to profile individual travelers.

Storage & retention: Session data lives in your active session; preferences are cleared when you log out or after inactivity. If you create an account, we store your email and hashed password for authentication.

Third parties: Media is fetched from Unsplash when an API key is configured; their requests are limited to destination keywords. We do not embed trackers or ad pixels.

Your controls: You can request deletion of your account data anytime by contacting support@sarathi.app. Avoid sharing sensitive personal data in freeform fields.

Security: Passwords are hashed with bcrypt; sessions are secured with HTTP-only cookies. We monitor for abuse and will refine safeguards continuously.
