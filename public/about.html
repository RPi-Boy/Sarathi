About Sarathi

We are a team of travelers, designers, and engineers crafting bespoke journeys with privacy at the center.

Sarathi began as a side-project on a layover in Singapore: a belief that AI could be as considerate as a trusted human planner. We stitched together taste-driven recommendations, mindful pacing, and logistics that respect your boundaries.

Every itinerary is generated with intention — balancing hidden gems, cultural depth, and restorative pauses. We aim to be the quiet co-pilot that adapts to your style, not the other way around.

Our commitment: keep your preferences yours, be transparent about how we generate suggestions, and constantly refine Sarathi with feedback from real travelers.
